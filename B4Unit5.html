Unit 5

從「感覺起來」到「使某人做事」

以北歐家具風格的清爽資訊層級，整理連綴動詞、感官動詞、使役動詞與 help 的完整句型。

05Linking · Perception · Causative

5-1 表感覺的連綴動詞

look、smell、taste、sound、feel 本身不能表達完整意思，後面常接形容詞作主詞補語。

基礎練習
Core Pattern

連綴動詞＋形容詞

  • 連綴動詞會依主詞與時態變化。
  • be 動詞也是最常見的連綴動詞。
主詞 + look / smell / taste / sound / feel + 形容詞
The sofa looks comfortable.
I am hungry.
Adjective or Adverb

怎麼判斷？

The classroom sounds noisy.
The students talked noisily.
描述主詞狀態
連綴動詞後使用形容詞
描述動作方式
一般動詞後多使用副詞
Practice A｜請選擇正確的形容詞或副詞
1. The sofa looks (comfortable / comfortably). You can sit in it (comfortable / comfortably).
comfortable / comfortably
2. The students are taking the test (nervous / nervously) because they feel very (nervous / nervously).
nervously / nervous
3. The students talked (noisy / noisily), and the classroom sounds (noisy / noisily).
noisily / noisy
4. This story sounds (happy / happily). The kids laugh (happy / happily).
happy / happily

5-2 連綴動詞 + like + 名詞

表感覺的連綴動詞後接 like + 名詞，可表達「看／聽／聞／嚐／摸起來像……」。

基礎練習
Pattern

基本句型

主詞 + look / smell / taste / sound / feel + like + 名詞
That movie sounds like an interesting one.
Your sister looks like a foreigner.
Practice B｜請重組下列句子
1. chicken. / The frog / tastes / like
The frog tastes like chicken.
2. dead / Your socks / fish. / smell / like
Your socks smell like dead fish.
3. My feet / rocks. / like / feel
My feet feel like rocks.
4. looked / a / The student / scary cat. / like
The student looked like a scary cat.
5. cotton candy. / The pillow / like / feels
The pillow feels like cotton candy.

5-3 表示狀態改變的連綴動詞

get、become、go、grow、turn 可表示狀態改變，並可搭配進行式表達正在改變的過程。

基礎練習
Change

狀態改變句型

  • 後面可接形容詞原級或比較級。
  • 也可用「比較級 + and + 比較級」表示變得越來越……。
  • become 表「成為」時，後面也可接名詞。
The leaves are becoming yellow.
I am getting hungrier and hungrier.
Paul became a baseball player.
Remember

連綴動詞後仍接形容詞

不要因為動詞在前面，就一律使用副詞；要先判斷是在補充主詞狀態，還是修飾動作。

狀態
He got angry.
動作方式
He looked at them angrily.
Practice C｜請圈選正確的形容詞或副詞
1. Computers are becoming more and more (important / importantly).
important
2. My grandfather gets (tired / tiredly) easily.
tired
3. Life is getting more and more (comfortable / comfortably) for them.
comfortable
4. My grandmother has become very (weak / weakly), and she walks (weak / weakly).
weak / weakly
5. He got (angry / angrily), and he looked at them (angry / angrily).
angry / angrily

5-4 表示狀態保持的連綴動詞

keep、stay 可表達狀態持續，後面接形容詞作補語。

基礎練習
Keep a State

keep / stay + 形容詞

主詞 + keep / stay + 形容詞
Peggy likes to keep busy.
Try to stay calm when an earthquake happens.
Practice D｜請依提示翻譯
1. 我妹妹一直高興了好幾天。(stay)
My sister stayed happy for many days.
2. 我爺爺需要保持健康。(stay)
My grandfather needs to stay healthy.
3. 喝熱湯能讓你保持溫暖。(keep)
Eating hot soup can keep you warm.
4. 請不要一直對我生氣；對你的健康不好。(stay)
Please don’t stay angry at me. It’s not good for your health.
5. 大家，保持安靜。(以 keep 開頭)
Keep quiet, everybody.

5-5 連綴動詞的 how / what 問句

how 問狀態如何；what...like 問看、聽、嚐、摸或聞起來像什麼。

基礎練習
How

問「感覺如何」

How + do / does / did + 主詞 + 連綴動詞原形 ...?
How do you feel? → I feel happy.
What...like

問「像什麼」

What + do / does / did + 主詞 + 連綴動詞原形 + like?
What does it look like? → It looks like a cat.
Practice E｜請依提示造原問句
1. The little lion looks like a cat.
What does the little lion look like?
2. Patty looks busy.
How does Patty look?
3. The coffee tasted like water.
What did the coffee taste like?
4. The fish tastes like chicken.
What does the fish taste like?
5. The cake smells strange.
How does the cake smell?
Practice F｜請填入 what 或 how
1. ___ do the boots feel? They feel comfortable.
How
2. ___ did the spaghetti taste? It tasted delicious.
How
3. ___ did the fish taste like? It tasted like pork.
What
4. ___ does my right hand feel like? It feels like ice.
What
5. ___ did the title of the story sound? It sounded sad.
How

5-6 感官動詞

see、watch、look at、hear、listen to、feel、smell 後可接原形動詞或 V-ing，語意焦點不同。

進階練習
Perception

受詞 + 原形動詞 / V-ing

感官動詞 + 受詞 + 原形動詞感官動詞 + 受詞 + V-ing
Jack saw him play / playing basketball.
Common Verbs

常見感官動詞

視覺
see / watch / look at
聽覺
hear / listen to
知覺與嗅覺
feel / smell
Practice G｜請依提示改寫句子
1. The bus is moving.（加入 No one feels...）
No one feels the bus moving.
2. A cat is running after a rat.（加入 Do you see...）
Do you see a cat running after a rat?
3. The girl is playing the piano in the music room.（加入 I see...）
I see the girl playing the piano in the music room.
4. The boys played baseball in the park.（加入 I saw...）
I saw the boys play baseball in the park.
5. The desks are shaking.（加入 Do you feel...）
Do you feel the desks shaking?

5-7 使役動詞

make、have、let 表示要求、使某人做事或允許某人做事，後接原形動詞。

進階練習
Causative

make / have / let

  • make 與 have 都可表命令或使某人做事，make 強制性較高。
  • let 表示允許。
  • 否定句是在使役動詞前加助動詞與 not。
主詞 + make / have / let + 受詞 + 原形動詞
Jack made him play basketball.
Jack won’t make him play basketball.
No to

使役動詞後不加 to

make / have / let 後面的動詞使用原形，不使用 to + V。

Practice H｜請依提示改寫
1. Mom had Nancy do her homework.（改為 Did 開頭）
Did Mom have Nancy do her homework?
2. I make him clean the living room.（加入 won’t）
I won’t make him clean the living room.
3. My dad had me go to the concert.（加入 didn’t）
My dad didn’t have me go to the concert.
4. He’ll make us happy.（加入 feel）
He’ll make us feel happy.
5. Sharon makes her brother angry.（加入 always）
Sharon always makes her brother angry.
Practice I｜請填入適當的動詞
1. Andrew always ___ (make) me ___ (laugh).
makes / laugh
2. My father ___ (let) me ___ (ride) a bicycle in the yard yesterday.
let / ride
3. Mike won’t ___ (let) his brother ___ (walk) to school alone every day.
let / walk
4. Mom ___ (have) me ___ (wash) the dishes last night.
had / wash
5. The teacher didn’t ___ (have) her students ___ (write) down the information.
have / write

5-8 動詞 help

help 後可接原形動詞或 to + 原形動詞；ask、tell 則接 to + V，否定時用 not to + V。

進階練習
Help

help + 受詞 + (to) V

help + 受詞 + 原形動詞help + 受詞 + to + 原形動詞
He helped me (to) fix the lamp.
He didn’t help me (to) fix the lamp.
Ask / Tell

ask / tell + 受詞 + to V

ask / tell + 受詞 + to + 原形動詞ask / tell + 受詞 + not to + 原形動詞
Please tell him to stay here.
My parents always ask me not to watch TV.
Practice J｜請填入適當的單字（每字限用一次）
1. She didn’t help me ___ the dishes.
do
2. Can you ___ me ___ the car?
help / fix
3. She ___ me to make a cake last week.
helped
4. Patty helped me ___ a card yesterday.
make
5. My sister doesn’t help Mom ___ some food.
to buy
Practice K｜請圈選正確的字
1. Please ask Patty (to clean / clean / cleaning) the dining room.
to clean
2. Mark told me not (to say / say / saying) so.
to say
3. Please help me (to do / doing) the laundry.
to do
4. My mother asked me (not play / not to play / playing) too many video games.
not to play
5. I can’t help you (writing / write) the answers.
write
