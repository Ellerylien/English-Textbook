10-1 對等連接詞簡介

對等連接詞連接相同文法地位的字、片語或子句。and 表一致，or 表選擇，but 表轉折。

基礎練習
Overview

and / or / but

  • and：連接相同或並列內容。
  • or：提出選擇。
  • but：表示前後對比或轉折。
He is a teacher and a doctor.
He is smart but lazy.
Would you like milk or tea?
Note

平行結構

連接詞兩端應盡量使用相同詞性或相同句型，閱讀會更清楚。

Practice A｜請填入正確的對等連接詞
1. He is a teacher ___ a doctor.
and
2. He is smart ___ lazy.
but
3. She is poor, ___ she is happy.
but
4. Scott studied hard, ___ he got good grades.
and
5. What would you like to drink, milk ___ tea?
or

10-2 both A and B

both A and B 表示「A 和 B 兩者都」，可連接名詞、形容詞或動詞。

基礎練習
Rule

both...and

both + 名詞 A + and + 名詞 Bboth + 形容詞 A + and + 形容詞 Bboth + 動詞 A + and + 動詞 B
Both John and Mary are teachers.
John is both hungry and thirsty.
John can both ride a bike and drive a car.
Note

與 not 連用

She doesn’t like both dogs and cats. 表示並非兩種都喜歡，可能只喜歡其中一種。

Practice B｜請依提示造句
1. both / Sandy / Sally / nurse
Both Sandy and Sally are nurses.
2. Tina / both / angry / sad
Tina is both angry and sad.
3. both / Helen / Harry / student
Both Helen and Harry are students.
4. Peter / can / both / swim / run
Peter can both swim and run.
5. He / not / like / both / spiders / frogs
He doesn’t like both spiders and frogs.
6. Michelle / like / both / red / blue
Michelle likes both red and blue.

10-3 either A or B

either A or B 表示兩者之中任一個；當整組當主詞時，動詞與最靠近的主詞一致。

基礎練習
Rule

either...or

either A or BEither A or B + 動詞（就近一致）
They can either stay home or go out.
Either you or he is a teacher.
Note

句尾的 either

either 單獨放在否定句句尾，表示「也不」：He is not a teacher, either.

Practice C｜請寫出正確的動詞
1. Either he or she ___ (be) a doctor.
is
2. Either he or they ___ (be) teachers.
are
3. They ___ (like) either lions or tigers.
like
4. ___ (be) either they or you lawyers?
Are
5. He ___ (like) either swimming or jogging.
likes
6. She ___ (want) either milk or juice.
wants

10-4 neither A nor B

neither A nor B 表示「A、B 兩者都不」，本身已含否定，不再搭配 not。動詞同樣採就近一致。

基礎練習
Rule

neither...nor

neither A nor BNeither A nor B + 動詞（就近一致）
John can neither ride a bike nor drive a car.
Neither you nor she is a teacher.
Note

同義改寫

neither...nor = not...either...or。

Practice D｜請合併句子
1. She isn’t a clerk. He isn’t a clerk, either.
Neither she nor he is a clerk.
2. I can’t swim. Peter can’t swim, either.
Neither I nor Peter can swim.
3. I don’t like drinking juice. I don’t like eating soup, either.
I like neither drinking juice nor eating soup.
4. He doesn’t like frogs. She doesn’t like frogs, either.
Neither he nor she likes frogs.
5. She can’t play the piano. She can’t play the violin, either.
She can neither play the piano nor play the violin.

10-5 not only...but also...

not only A but also B 表示「不但 A，而且 B」。連接主詞時，動詞與後面的主詞一致。

基礎練習
Rule

not only...but also

not only + A + but also + BNot only A but also B + 動詞（與 B 一致）
She is not only beautiful but also smart.
Not only you but also she is hard-working.
Note

平行詞性

A 與 B 必須保持相同詞性，例如兩個形容詞、兩個名詞或兩個動詞。

Practice E｜請翻譯下列句子
1. Paul 不但聰明而且勤奮。
Paul is not only smart but also hard-working.
2. Allen 不但是科學家而且是醫師。
Allen is not only a scientist but also a doctor.
3. 不但 Helen 去派對，而且 Debby 也去了派對。
Not only Helen but also Debby went to the party.
4. Andrew 不但是誠實的學生，而且是個好兒子。
Andrew is not only an honest student but also a good son.
5. Angelina 不但漂亮而且活潑。
Angelina is not only pretty but also active.

10-6 as well as / not only...but also

as well as 表示「以及、也」，連接兩個主詞時，動詞通常與前面的主詞一致；可和 not only...but also 互換。

進階練習
Rule

as well as

主詞 + be + A + as well as + B主詞 A + as well as + 主詞 B + 動詞（與 A 一致）
She is beautiful as well as smart.
John as well as you went to the amusement park.
Note

主詞一致比較

not only A but also B
動詞與 B 一致
A as well as B
動詞與 A 一致
Practice F｜互換 as well as 與 not only...but also
1. Mike is not only handsome but also hard-working.
Mike is handsome as well as hard-working.
2. John is not only a scientist but also a doctor.
John is a scientist as well as a doctor.
3. Not only John but also you went to the amusement park.
John as well as you went to the amusement park.
4. Andy is not only an active boy but also a good student.
Andy is an active boy as well as a good student.
5. Angel is not only honest but also smart.
Angel is honest as well as smart.

10-7 but / although

but 與 although 都能表達轉折，但不能同時放在同一句中。

進階練習
Rule

兩種表達方式

Although + 子句, 主要子句主要子句, but + 主要子句
Although she was sick, she still went to school.
She was sick, but she still went to school.
Note

常見錯誤

不要寫成 Although..., but...；二者選一即可。

Practice G｜填入 although 或 but
1. ___ he is young, he is very smart.
Although
2. He is old, ___ he is very healthy.
but
3. She is not proud ___ she is very smart.
although
4. I smiled at Mike, ___ he didn’t smile back.
but
5. He was not happy ___ he passed the examination.
although
6. ___ I like her, I am afraid to tell her.
Although
Practice H｜互換 but 與 although
1. Although Mike slept late, he didn’t feel tired.
Mike slept late, but he didn’t feel tired.
2. Although she is beautiful, she is dishonest.
She is beautiful, but she is dishonest.
3. Although we had a chance to win a special prize, we lost it.
We had a chance to win a special prize, but we lost it.
4. Although it rains, we still don’t have enough water.
It rains, but we still don’t have enough water.
5. Although she wore a sweater, she still felt cold.
She wore a sweater, but she still felt cold.

10-8 so / because

because 引導原因；so 引導結果。二者不能同時出現在同一句中。

進階練習
Rule

原因與結果的互換

Because + 原因, 結果原因, so + 結果
Because he is nice to me, I like him.
He is nice to me, so I like him.
Note

句型方向

because 後面
原因
so 後面
結果
Practice I｜填入 because 或 so
1. ___ he studied hard, he got better grades.
Because
2. He won the lottery, ___ he was very happy.
so
3. She is happy ___ she gets to vote when she’s twenty.
because
4. Kitty lost the game, ___ she is sad.
so
5. ___ they saw a sad movie, they cried.
Because
Practice J｜互換 because 與 so
1. She learned English very well because she listened to English songs.
She listened to English songs, so she learned English very well.
2. He always gets good grades because he has good study habits.
He has good study habits, so he always gets good grades.
3. I did the best on the English report because I got some useful tips.
I got some useful tips, so I did the best on the English report.
4. Angela failed the math quiz because she didn’t do exercises.
Angela didn’t do exercises, so she failed the math quiz.
5. Mark couldn’t play basketball because his arm hurt.
Mark’s arm hurt, so he couldn’t play basketball.
