Unit 9

讓句子表達時間、原因與條件

從 when、before、because、if、until，到 so...that、unless 等進階結構，整理英文複句的邏輯關係。

09Subordinators · Conditions · Results

9-1 從屬連接詞簡介

從屬連接詞引導的子句不能單獨表達完整意思；從屬子句在句首時，通常以逗號和主要子句隔開。

基礎練習
Overview

從屬子句與主要子句

  • 從屬子句可放在主要子句之前或之後。
  • 從屬子句放句首時，後面要加逗號。
  • 常見連接詞：when、before、after、until、because、although、if。
Jason listened to music when he studied English.
When Jason studied English, he listened to music.
Note

標點提醒

從屬子句在後
通常不加逗號
從屬子句在前
子句後加逗號
Practice A｜把從屬子句移到句首
1. Please buy me some snacks when you come back.
When you come back, please buy me some snacks.
2. I drink warm milk before I go to bed.
Before I go to bed, I drink warm milk.
3. She didn’t cry although she felt very sad.
Although she felt very sad, she didn’t cry.
4. You may get hurt if you play tennis too much.
If you play tennis too much, you may get hurt.

9-2 從屬連接詞 when

when 表示某事發生的時間點。談未來時，when 子句仍使用現在式。

基礎練習
Rule

when 的時態搭配

現在式 + when + 現在式過去式 + when + 過去式未來式 + when + 現在式祈使句 + when + 現在式
I will buy a gift when I have enough money.
Note

未來時間子句

主要子句可以用 will，但 when 後面的子句不用 will，要用現在式代替未來。

Practice B｜請用 when 合併句子
1. She cries. / She is very sad.
She cries when she is very sad.
2. The baby is quiet. / He goes to the church.
The baby is quiet when he goes to the church.
3. Don’t eat food in the living room. / You watch TV.
Don’t eat food in the living room when you watch TV.
4. I will buy a gift for my brother. / I have enough money.
I will buy a gift for my brother when I have enough money.
5. Kitty looked very happy. / She came back from school.
Kitty looked very happy when she came back from school.
6. Please turn off the light. / You leave the room.
Please turn off the light when you leave the room.

9-3 after / before

after、before 和 when 一樣，可引導表示先後順序的時間子句；也可以當介系詞，後面接名詞或動名詞。

基礎練習
Rule

連接詞用法

主要子句 + before / after + 主詞 + 動詞Before / After + 主詞 + 動詞, 主要子句
I drink some milk before I go to bed.
Open the window after you enter the house.
Note

介系詞用法

before / after + 名詞before / after + V-ing
He bought a present before going to the party.
Practice C｜依提示合併句子
1. Andy eats breakfast at 6 a.m. / Andy goes to school at 7 a.m. (before)
Andy eats breakfast before he goes to school.
2. I will cook dinner for Mom. / Mom comes home. (before)
I will cook dinner for Mom before she comes home.
3. She drank milk at 10 p.m. / She went to bed at 11 p.m. (after)
She went to bed after she drank milk.
4. You wash your hands. / You eat dinner. (after)
Eat dinner after you wash your hands.
Practice D｜把連接詞改成介系詞
1. He bought a present before he went to Andy’s birthday party.
He bought a present before going to Andy’s birthday party.
2. I drink milk before I go to bed.
I drink milk before going to bed.
3. My mother will visit my grandfather after she goes shopping.
My mother will visit my grandfather after going shopping.
4. Kitty finished her homework before she took a shower.
Kitty finished her homework before taking a shower.
5. Don’t talk after you enter the room.
Don’t talk after entering the room.

9-4 although / though

although、though、even though 表示讓步：「雖然……但是……」。正式書寫通常以 although 為主。

基礎練習
Rule

讓步子句

Although / Though + 子句, 主要子句主要子句 + although / though + 子句
Although she studied hard, she got poor grades.
Note

though 的位置

  • though 較常見於口語。
  • though 可以放句首，也可放句尾。
  • although 與 but 不可同時放在同一句中。
Practice E｜請重組句子
1. sent us / every day / My mother / to school / though / she is busy.
My mother sent us to school every day though she is busy.
2. his boss / Although / works hard, / doesn’t like / him. / he
Although he works hard, his boss doesn’t like him.
3. he worked / Though / was tired, / very late. / Mark
Though Mark was tired, he worked very late.
4. I / sad. / didn’t cry / although / I was
I didn’t cry although I was sad.
5. She didn’t / very sick. / see a doctor / although / go to / she felt
She didn’t go to see a doctor although she felt very sick.

9-5 because

because 引導原因子句，表達「因為……所以……」的因果關係；英文中不和 so 同時使用。

基礎練習
Rule

原因子句

Because + 原因子句, 結果子句結果子句 + because + 原因子句
Because she studied hard, she got good grades.
Note

although 與 because

although
雖然：讓步
because
因為：原因
Practice F｜填入 although 或 because
1. ___ she was so hungry, she had a big dinner.
Because
2. ___ she was tired, she worked ten hours today.
Although
3. My father came to my school fair ___ he was ill.
although
4. John didn’t have classes yesterday ___ it was a holiday.
because
5. She goes to bed early every day ___ she has to get up before dawn.
because

9-6 if 條件子句

if 引導條件子句，主要子句表達條件成立後的結果。談未來時，if 子句用現在式。

基礎練習
Rule

第一條件句

If + 現在式, 主詞 + will / can / may + 原形動詞主詞 + will / can / may + 原形動詞 + if + 現在式
If you turn left at the first block, you will see the toy shop.
Note

祈使句也能當結果

If you feel tired, go to bed.
Practice G｜請寫出正確的動詞
1. If the weather ___ (be) nice tomorrow, they ___ (go) hiking.
is / will go
2. If you ___ (want) to gain weight, you should eat more.
want
3. If you ___ (feel) tired, ___ (go) to bed.
feel / go
4. If Amy ___ (agree) to go to the movies, Mark will be very happy.
agrees
5. If Jay ___ (not study) hard, he will not get good grades.
doesn’t study
6. If you don’t work eight hours a day, your boss ___ (get) angry.
will get

9-7 until / not...until

until 可當連接詞或介系詞，表示「直到」；not...until 表示「直到……才」。

基礎練習
Rule

until 的用法

主要子句 + until + 現在式／過去式will + 原形動詞 + until + 現在式
He will stay until you get here.
My brother studied until Father came home.
Note

肯定與否定的差異

study until 11
持續讀書到十一點
not study until 11
直到十一點才開始讀書
Practice H｜請寫出正確的動詞
1. I ___ (study) math until Mom ___ (come) home.
studied / came
2. I will work until you ___ (finish) your job.
finish
3. Tom ___ (not be) here until it ___ (get) dark tomorrow.
won’t be / gets
4. I ___ (wait) at home until you come back.
will wait
5. She didn’t get into the classroom until they ___ (open) the door.
opened
6. The students kept talking until the teacher ___ (come).
came

9-8 since

since 可當連接詞或介系詞，表示「自從……」。主要子句常用現在完成式。

基礎練習
Rule

since 的兩種結構

have / has + p.p. + since + 過去式子句have / has + p.p. + since + 時間起點
I have learned English since I was six.
I have learned English since 2007.
Note

時態搭配

主要子句
現在完成式
since 子句
過去式
Practice I｜請寫出正確的動詞
1. They ___ (not see) Tom since they ___ (leave).
haven’t seen / left
2. Mr. Brown ___ (teach) math since he ___ (be) twenty-five.
has taught / was
3. He ___ (lose) weight since last month.
has lost
4. Tom ___ (play) the game since seven o’clock.
has played
5. Kitty ___ (live) in Taipei since she ___ (be) a child.
has lived / was
6. I ___ (clean) the living room since I ___ (come) home.
have cleaned / came

9-9 so...that...

so...that... 表示「如此……以致於……」。so 後接形容詞或副詞，that 後接完整子句。

基礎練習
Rule

結果句型

主詞 + be + so + 形容詞 + that + 子句主詞 + 一般動詞 + so + 副詞 + that + 子句
The boy is so young that he can’t go to school.
Note

形容詞或副詞？

be 動詞後
so + 形容詞
一般動詞後
so + 副詞
Practice J｜請合併句子
1. The girl is old. / She can go to school.
The girl is so old that she can go to school.
2. The baby was scared. / He cried.
The baby was so scared that he cried.
3. Mike is lazy. / He can’t get good grades.
Mike is so lazy that he can’t get good grades.
4. She is mean. / Nobody can work with her.
She is so mean that nobody can work with her.
5. Tom is honest. / I can trust him.
Tom is so honest that I can trust him.

9-10 so...that / enough to / too...to

肯定的 so...that...can 可改為 enough to；否定的 so...that...can’t 可改為 too...to。

進階練習
Rule

肯定句互換

so + 形容詞 + that + 主詞 + can + V形容詞 + enough + for + 人 + to + V
The book is so easy that I can read it.
The book is easy enough for me to read.
Rule

否定句互換

so + 形容詞 + that + 主詞 + can’t + Vtoo + 形容詞 + for + 人 + to + V
The boy is so short that he can’t play basketball.
The boy is too short to play basketball.
Practice K｜請翻譯並寫出兩種句型
1. 這個女孩太矮了，以致於不能騎腳踏車。
The girl is too short to ride a bike. / The girl is so short that she can’t ride a bike.
2. 英文對我而言太容易了，以致於我能閱讀。
English is easy enough for me to read. / English is so easy that I can read it.
3. 這個老人太老了，以致於不能開車。
This old man is too old to drive a car. / This old man is so old that he can’t drive a car.
4. 那件裙子對她而言太長了，以致於不能穿。
That skirt is too long for her to wear. / That skirt is so long that she can’t wear it.
Practice L｜請填入適當的字
1. Science is ___ hard for me to study.
too
2. Biology is interesting ___ for her to study.
enough
3. English is so boring for her ___ she can’t stay awake.
that
4. The name is too long for him ___ remember.
to
5. Chemistry is so ___ that I can’t study it.
hard

9-11 so...that / such...that

so 後面直接接形容詞；such 後面接 a/an + 形容詞 + 單數名詞。

進階練習
Rule

so...that

主詞 + be + so + 形容詞 + that + 子句
He is so nice that everybody likes him.
Rule

such...that

主詞 + be + such + a/an + 形容詞 + 名詞 + that + 子句
He is such a nice boy that everybody likes him.
Practice M｜請依提示改寫句子
1. Tina is such a cute girl that many people like her. (so...)
Tina is so cute that many people like her.
2. She is so pretty that she is very popular with the boys. (such...)
She is such a pretty girl that she is very popular with the boys.
3. Mike is so handsome that others envy him. (such...)
Mike is such a handsome boy that others envy him.
4. He is such a mean teacher that students don’t like him. (so...)
He is so mean that students don’t like him.
5. Kitty is such a hard-working student that she always gets good grades. (so...)
Kitty is so hard-working that she always gets good grades.

9-12 unless / if...not

unless 表示「除非」，通常等於 if...not。改寫時要注意否定只保留一次。

進階練習
Rule

unless

Unless + 肯定子句, 結果子句
Unless you study hard, you won’t pass the test.
Rule

if...not

If + 否定子句, 結果子句
If you don’t study hard, you won’t pass the test.
Practice N｜請依提示改寫句子
1. Unless it rains, there will not be enough water. (If...)
If it doesn’t rain, there will not be enough water.
2. Unless he comes back, he will not get gifts from me. (If...)
If he doesn’t come back, he will not get gifts from me.
3. If you don’t visit your grandparents, they will not be happy with you. (Unless...)
Unless you visit your grandparents, they will not be happy with you.
4. If he doesn’t work carefully, he will not get paid. (Unless...)
Unless he works carefully, he will not get paid.
5. Unless he is a teacher, he cannot teach us math. (If...)
If he is not a teacher, he cannot teach us math.
