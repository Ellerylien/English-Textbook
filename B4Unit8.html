Unit 8

把過去經驗連到現在

用清楚的時間線概念，整理 have / has + p.p.、already / yet、ever / never，以及持續時間的問答。

08Present Perfect · Experience · Duration

8-1 現在完成式的基本介紹

現在完成式使用 have / has + 過去分詞，描述完成、經驗與持續。

基礎練習
Rule

基本結構與動詞三態

  • 現在完成式使用過去分詞，不是過去式。
  • 規則動詞的 p.p. 通常與過去式相同；不規則動詞需特別記憶。
主詞 + have / has + p.p.
Jim has eaten lunch.
Note

常見不規則變化

A-B-C
know-knew-known / see-saw-seen
A-B-B
keep-kept-kept / buy-bought-bought
A-A-A
cut-cut-cut / put-put-put
i-a-u
swim-swam-swum / sing-sang-sung
Practice A｜請寫出動詞三態
1. come
come / came / come
2. go
go / went / gone
3. buy
buy / bought / bought
4. see
see / saw / seen
5. eat
eat / ate / eaten
6. do
do / did / done
7. run
run / ran / run
8. stop
stop / stopped / stopped
9. sell
sell / sold / sold
10. teach
teach / taught / taught
11. cut
cut / cut / cut
12. ask
ask / asked / asked
13. forget
forget / forgot / forgotten
14. know
know / knew / known

8-2 現在完成式句型與 already / yet

already 常用於肯定句；yet 常用於否定句與疑問句。

基礎練習
Rule

肯定、否定與疑問

主詞 + have / has + p.p.主詞 + have / has not + p.p.Have / Has + 主詞 + p.p. ...?
She has already done her homework.
She has not done her homework yet.
Has she done her homework yet?
Note

位置

already
have / has 與 p.p. 中間，或句尾
yet
通常置於疑問句或否定句句尾
Practice B｜請依提示改寫
1. I have read that novel.（already 放句中）
I have already read that novel.
2. They have eaten pizza.（already 放句尾）
They have eaten pizza already.
3. Have you been to America?（肯定簡答）
Yes, I have.
4. Sandy has drunk much wine.（yet 改否定）
Sandy hasn’t drunk much wine yet.
5. Kitty has already studied Lesson Five.（改疑問）
Has Kitty studied Lesson Five yet?
6. I have taught those students.（yet 改疑問）
Have you taught those students yet?

8-3 ever / before / once / twice / never

現在完成式可表達經驗，常搭配 ever、never、before、once、twice 等副詞。

基礎練習
Rule

經驗用法

  • ever 常放在 have / has 與 p.p. 之間。
  • once / twice / three times / before 通常置於句尾。
  • never 放在 have / has 後面。
Have you ever seen a 3D movie?
No, I never have.
Note

簡答形式

肯定
Yes, I have. / Yes, I’ve seen one.
否定
No, never. / No, I never have.
Practice C｜請依提示改寫
1. Yes, they have.（never 改否定回答）
No, they never have.
2. We have learned English.（加入 never）
We have never learned English.
3. Kitty has seen a falling star.（ever 改疑問）
Has Kitty ever seen a falling star?
4. Sandy and Sally have watched the show.（加入 twice）
Sandy and Sally have watched the show twice.
5. Has Tina ever ridden a horse before?（never 回答）
No, she never has.
6. She has never seen a giraffe.（加入 before）
She has never seen a giraffe before.

8-4 have gone to / have been to

have gone to 表示人去了某地、目前仍在那裡；have been to 表示曾經去過、目前不在那裡。

基礎練習
Rule

兩個句型的差異

Meg has gone to Taipei.（Meg 現在在台北）
Meg has been to Taipei.（Meg 曾去過，目前不在台北）
Note

經驗次數

have / has been to 可搭配 once、twice、three times、several times。

Practice D｜填入 have / has gone to 或 have / has been to
1. We ___ America. We are shopping now.
have gone to
2. I ___ London. It was fun.
have been to
3. Mary ___ Japan. She will be back tomorrow.
has gone to
4. She ___ America twice.
has been to
5. A: Is Sandy at home? B: No, she ___ France.
has gone to
6. Candy ___ Canada several times.
has been to

8-5 since / for

for 接一段時間；since 接時間起點，也可以接過去式子句。

基礎練習
Rule

持續時間的兩種表達

for + 一段時間since + 過去時間起點since + 過去式子句
Mr. Lin has taught English for ten years.
I haven’t seen John since 2010.
I haven’t written to Bruce since I left.
Note

判斷方式

for
回答「多久」：ten years / one hour
since
回答「從何時開始」：2010 / I was six
Practice E｜請填入 since 或 for
1. I have learned English ___ I was six.
since
2. She has worked ___ eight hours.
for
3. He has played the piano ___ four o’clock.
since
4. The window has been open ___ thirty minutes.
for
5. She has been in school ___ one hour.
for
6. Jay has danced ___ he was a child.
since
7. They haven’t bought a new car ___ 2010.
since

8-6 表持續的副詞

so far、recently、all day、all this year、all my life 常與現在完成式連用。

進階練習
Rule

常見副詞片語

  • so far：到目前為止
  • recently：最近
  • all day / all this year / all my life：整段時間持續
It has been OK so far.
How have you been recently?
Note

recently 的彈性

recently 也可搭配過去式，例如：I went there recently.

Practice F｜依提示用現在完成式造句
1. It / be / sunny / all day
It has been sunny all day.
2. Tina / learn / English very well / so far
Tina has learned English very well so far.
3. Candy and Andy / see / elephants / recently
Candy and Andy have seen elephants recently.
4. William / study / English / hard / all this year
William has studied English hard all this year.
5. Ken / teach / math / all his life
Ken has taught math all his life.
6. I / be / tired / all day
I have been tired all day.

8-7 現在完成式的 how 問句

How long 用來詢問某動作或狀態持續多久，回答通常搭配 for 或 since。

進階練習
Rule

How long 問答

How long + have / has + 主詞 + p.p. ...?主詞 + have / has + p.p. + for / since ...
How long has she lived in Taipei?
She has lived in Taipei for ten years.
Note

since 子句

She has lived in Taipei since she was little.
Since she was little, she has lived in Taipei.
Practice G｜請依提示回答
1. How long have you learned English?（六年）
I have learned English for six years.
2. How long has your brother taken piano lessons?（從去年起）
He has taken piano lessons since last year.
3. How long have they had breakfast?（一小時）
They have had breakfast for one hour.
4. How long has Peter written a letter?（兩小時）
He has written a letter for two hours.
5. How long have you read that novel?（從昨天起）
I have read that novel since yesterday.
6. How long has Candy worked in that company?（從她搬去台北）
She has worked in that company since she moved to Taipei.

8-8 現在完成式與 just

just 表示「剛剛」，放在 have / has 與 p.p. 之間；just now 則搭配過去式並放句尾。

進階練習
Rule

just 與 just now

I have just come back.
I came back just now.
Note

快速判斷

just
現在完成式：have / has just + p.p.
just now
過去式：V-ed / irregular past + just now
Practice H｜請依提示改寫
1. Kitty has finished her homework.（加入 just）
Kitty has just finished her homework.
2. Lily has just taken a shower.（改用 just now）
Lily took a shower just now.
3. They have had lunch.（加入 just）
They have just had lunch.
4. Candy took a test just now.（改用 just）
Candy has just taken a test.
5. I have just seen a movie.（改用 just now）
I saw a movie just now.
6. Jane and Jenny bought new books just now.（改用 just）
Jane and Jenny have just bought new books.
