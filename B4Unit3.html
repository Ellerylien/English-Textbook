3-1 情態副詞變化

掌握形容詞變成情態副詞的基本規則，是本單元的起點。

基礎練習
Rule

形容詞 → 副詞規則整理

變化方式說明與例字
直接加 -lybad → badly
quiet → quietly
careful → carefully
字尾是 -le：去 e 加 -yterrible → terribly
comfortable → comfortably
字尾是「子音 + y」：去 y 加 -ilyeasy → easily
happy → happily
hungry → hungrily
Note

補充提醒

  • 有些字雖然字尾也是 -ly，但其實是形容詞，例如 friendly。
  • 有些副詞屬於不規則或同形變化：late → late / hard → hard / early → early / fast → fast / high → high / good → well。
核心觀念
副詞多半修飾動詞，描述「如何做」
易混淆字
friendly 不是副詞，而是形容詞
Practice A｜請將形容詞改成副詞
1. comfortable
comfortably
2. loud
loudly
3. polite
politely
4. angry
angrily
5. good
well
6. fast
fast
7. high
high
8. easy
easily

3-2 情態副詞原形

副詞主要修飾動詞，形容詞則修飾名詞。也要留意副詞在句中的常見位置。

基礎練習
Compare

副詞與形容詞的差別

Mandy is dancing happily. Mandy 正開心地跳舞。
Mandy is a happy girl. Mandy 是個快樂的女孩。
Position

情態副詞的位置

位置例句
常見於句尾He wrote the letter carefully. 他仔細地寫這封信。
大部分可置於動詞前後I looked at the teacher nervously.
= I nervously looked at the teacher.
不規則同形副詞多置於動詞後Tom got up late.
Practice B｜請依提示填入形容詞或副詞
1. Mr. Wu drives very ___ (fast). He is a ___ (fast) driver.
fast / fast
2. Mrs. White is a ___ (sad) woman. She talks ___ (sad).
sad / sadly
3. Birds fly ___ (high), so the nest is ___ (high) in the tree.
high / high
4. He is ___ (late) for school. He goes to school ___ (late).
late / late
5. Mr. Wang is a ___ (famous) writer, but he is not a ___ (famous) speaker.
famous / famous

3-3 as + 副詞原形 + as

副詞和形容詞一樣，可以用 as...as 表示同等比較。否定句常搭配 not ... as / so ... as。

基礎練習
Formula

肯定句

A + 一般動詞 + as + 副詞原形 + as + B
She works as hard as Amy. 她和 Amy 一樣辛勤工作。
Formula

否定句

A + 助動詞 + not + 一般動詞 + as / so + 副詞原形 + as + B
She doesn’t work so hard as Amy. 她沒有和 Amy 一樣辛勤工作。
Practice C｜請依提示造句
1. He / can run / fast / I
He can run as fast as I.
2. I can’t / go / often / you
I can’t go so often as you.
3. She / can’t / write / well / my sister
She can’t write as well as my sister.
4. They / didn’t walk / far / I
They didn’t walk as far as I.
5. He / learns / quickly / Susan
He learns as quickly as Susan.

3-4 情態副詞 how 問句

疑問詞 how 可以問「狀況如何」，若問「做得多好」則用 how well。回答時常用情態副詞。

基礎練習
Example

問句與回答

How does Amy do her homework? → She does her homework very carelessly.
How well does Kevin play tennis? → He plays tennis very well.
Hint

快速記憶

  • 問「怎麼做」：How + 助動詞 / be + 主詞 + V ...?
  • 問「做得多好」：How well + 助動詞 + 主詞 + V ...?
Practice D｜請依提示造原問句
1. She teaches English happily.
How does she teach English?
2. The girl is looking at him angrily.
How is the girl looking at him?
3. The singer sang English songs well.
How did the singer sing English songs?
4. Tom dances very well.
How does Tom dance?
Practice E｜請依提示回答
1. How does Jeff hold a glass bottle? (careful)
He holds a glass bottle carefully.
2. How are Kitty and Lily talking to each other? (happy)
They are talking to each other happily.
3. How is Emily doing at school? (good)
She is doing well at school.
4. How does Mr. White work every day? (hard)
He works hard every day.

3-5 情態副詞比較級

副詞也可以做比較級，用來比較兩者做事情的方式。

基礎練習
Rule

比較級形成

  • 與形容詞同形者：字尾加 -er，如 faster / harder。
  • 由形容詞加 -ly 形成的副詞：前面加 more，如 more carefully / more slowly。
  • 不規則：well → better / badly → worse / far → farther / further。
Mike studies harder than his classmates.
Practice F｜請將副詞原級改成比較級
1. beautifully
more beautifully
2. clearly
more clearly
3. far
farther / further
4. little
less
5. bravely
more bravely
6. much
more
7. well
better
8. quickly
more quickly
Practice G｜請合併成副詞比較級的句子
1. Andy’s dog dances well. / Eddie’s dog dances better.
Eddie’s dog dances better than Andy’s.
2. Scott does his homework slowly. / William does his homework more slowly.
William does his homework more slowly than Scott.
3. I can’t walk as fast as you. / I walk slowly.
You walk faster than I.
4. Sue didn’t write as carefully as Lisa. / Lisa wrote very carefully.
Lisa wrote more carefully than Sue.
5. Ben lives far from the school. / Will lives farther from the school.
Will lives farther from the school than Ben.
6. Kate said something about the problem. / Betty said more about the problem.
Betty said further about the problem than Kate.

3-6 副詞比較級 + and + 副詞比較級

表示「越來越⋯⋯」時，可用比較級 + and + 比較級，或 more and more + 副詞。

基礎練習
Pattern

句型說明

Jack is playing tennis better and better. Jack 的網球打得越來越好。
His father walks faster and faster in the park.
Practice H｜請依提示改寫句子
1. Melody is playing the piano ___ (good)
better and better
2. Mark studied ___ (hard)
harder and harder
3. He writes ___ (careful)
more and more carefully
4. The eagle is flying ___ in the sky. (high)
higher and higher

3-7 than any / than all the / than any other / than all the other

比較級也能表達最高級概念。若比較對象與自己同群體，要記得加 other。

進階練習
Pattern

常見結構

Jack is playing tennis better than any other boy in the class.
Practice I｜請依提示改寫
1. Turtles run the most slowly in the zoo. (any other animal...)
Turtles run more slowly than any other animal in the zoo.
2. Kitty sings the most beautifully in the class. (any other...)
Kitty sings more beautifully than any other girl in the class.
3. The baby laughs the most happily in the room. (all the other...)
The baby laughs more happily than all the other babies in the room.
4. Helen plays the drums the most loudly in the band. (all the other...)
Helen plays the drums more loudly than all the other people in the band.

3-8 情態副詞最高級：of all / in + 地方

三者以上的比較要使用最高級。副詞的最高級常搭配 of all 或 in + 地方。

進階練習
Rule

最高級形成

  • 同形副詞：字尾加 -est，如 fastest / hardest。
  • 以 -ly 結尾的副詞：前面加 most，如 most carefully / most slowly。
  • 不規則：farther/further → the farthest / furthest、little → the least。
Jack runs the fastest in the class.
Practice J｜請將副詞比較級改成最高級
1. more carefully
the most carefully
2. little
the least
3. faster
the fastest
4. earlier
the earliest
5. more comfortably
the most comfortably
6. farther
the farthest / furthest
7. more bravely
the most bravely
8. more carefully
the most carefully
Practice K｜請合併下列句子
1. Ken works 10 hours a day. / Kevin works 12 hours a day. / Kitty works 13 hours a day. (hard)
Kitty works the hardest.
2. Jill plays the piano well. / Jenny plays the piano better than Jill. / Jane plays the piano better than Jenny. (well)
Jane plays the piano the best.
3. Jeff jumps farther than Paul. / Paul jumps farther than Peter. (far)
Jeff jumps the farthest.
4. I have less money than Kate. / Kate has less money than Tina. (less)
I have the least money.
5. Amy ate more than Annie. / Annie ate more than Andy. (much)
Amy ate the most.

3-9 情態副詞表最高級的句型

兩者比較與三者（以上）比較的結構不同，要分辨 of the two 和 of the + 數量。

進階練習
Sentence Pattern

句型比較

副詞最高級的句型副詞比較級的句型
用在三者（以上）
the + 副詞最高級 + of the + 數量
the best of the three		用在兩者
the + 副詞比較級 + of the two
the better of the two
Example

例句

He plays the best of the three. 他是三者中打得最好的。
He plays the better of the two. 他是兩者中打得比較好的。
Practice L｜請依提示造句
1. He / jumps / far / two
He jumps the farther of the two.
2. The man / walked / fast / five
The man walked the fastest of the five.
3. The girl / is dancing / beautiful / three
The girl is dancing the most beautifully of the three.
4. Susan / wrote / careless / six
Susan wrote the most carelessly of the six.
5. Robert / got up / early / four
Robert got up the earliest of the four.
