7-1 情態助動詞的概念

情態助動詞幫助主要動詞表達能力、義務、確定性與可能性。

基礎練習
Overview

情態助動詞的核心概念

  • 情態助動詞後面接原形動詞。
  • could、might、would 可作過去式，也能用來表達較委婉的語氣。
  • 確定性：will / can / must；可能性由強到弱常見 must → may / can → might / could。
He can dance.
She must leave now.
He might be sick.
Note

可能性的強弱

強烈推測
must：一定
一般可能
may / can：可能
較弱可能
might / could：也許
Practice A｜請重組句子
1. could / He / swim.
He could swim.
2. here? / May / smoke / I
May I smoke here?
3. She / here. / come / should
She should come here.
4. hard. / I / work / must
I must work hard.
5. back / They / come / should / home.
They should come back home.
6. me? / you / help / Would
Would you help me?

7-2 can / could

can 表現在或未來的能力；could 可表過去能力，也可表較弱的可能性。

基礎練習
Rule

能力與可能性

can + 原形動詞could + 原形動詞
He couldn’t swim then, but he can now.
It could be hot tomorrow.
Note

時態提醒

現在／未來能力
can / can’t
過去能力
could / couldn’t
推測
can’t：不可能；could：可能
Practice B｜填入 can / can’t / could / couldn’t
1. I ___ (not) walk when I was one year old.
couldn’t
2. She ___ speak English three years ago, but she ___ now.
couldn’t / can
3. I ___ swim now but ___ before.
can’t / could
4. She ___ be here yesterday.
couldn’t
5. He ___ be sick now.
can’t
6. A: ___ you do magic tricks? B: Yes, I ___.
Can / can
7. You ___ write a story well.
can’t

7-3 may / might

may 與 might 可表現在或未來的可能性，也能用於請求允許；may 通常較正式。

基礎練習
Rule

may / might 的兩種功能

  • 表可能：may 的可能性通常比 might 稍高。
  • 表允許：may 比 can 正式。
  • 否定為 may not / might not，通常不縮寫。
May I dance in the living room?
I might take a train to Taipei.
Note

快速比較

may
可能、可以；較正式
might
可能性較弱；語氣更保留
Practice C｜請依提示翻譯
1. 我可以在客廳跳舞嗎？
May / Might I dance in the living room?
2. 你不可以晚上獨自出門。
You may not / might not go out alone at night.
3. 我可能搭乘火車去台北。
I may / might take a train to Taipei.
4. Jeff 可能買一台相機嗎？不，不可能。
May / Might Jeff buy a camera? No, he may / might not.

7-4 must / have to

must 表義務或強烈推測；過去的「必須」要用 had to，過去的推測則用 must have + p.p.。

基礎練習
Rule

義務、禁止與推測

must + 原形動詞have / has to + 原形動詞had to + 原形動詞must have + p.p.
You must not enter the house.
You had to finish your homework last night.
She must have been sick last week.
Note

must not 不等於 don’t have to

must not
強烈禁止：不可以做
don’t have to
不必做；可以做也可以不做
Practice D｜請依提示改寫
1. She must do her homework. (not)
She must not do her homework.
2. You must type the letter carefully. (have to)
You have to type the letter carefully.
3. It must rain a lot. (yesterday)
It must have rained a lot yesterday.
4. You must buy the uniform. (have to...yesterday)
You had to buy the uniform yesterday.
5. Tina must have been sick yesterday. (today)
Tina must be sick today.

7-5 should / had better

should 與 had better 都可表示建議；should have + p.p. 表示「過去本來應該，但事實沒有」。

基礎練習
Rule

建議與事實相反

should + 原形動詞had better + 原形動詞had better not + 原形動詞should have + p.p.
She should leave. = She had better leave.
She should have come here yesterday.
Note

語氣差異

should
一般建議
had better
較強烈、常暗示不照做會有後果
should have + p.p.
過去應該做卻沒做
Practice E｜請依提示改寫
1. It should have snowed here last night. (today)
It should snow here today.
2. She should mail the letter for me. (had better)
She had better mail the letter for me.
3. He should be sick today. (yesterday)
He should have been sick yesterday.
4. You had better write the words carefully. (should)
You should write the words carefully.
5. Sandy and Mandy should come and join us. (had better)
Sandy and Mandy had better come and join us.
6. Students had better study hard. (should)
Students should study hard.

7-6 should / ought to

should 與 ought to 都表示「應該」；ought to 是情態概念中少數保留 to 的結構。

基礎練習
Rule

同義句型

should + 原形動詞ought to + 原形動詞
You should wash the dishes. = You ought to wash the dishes.
Note

情態後面的 to

不加 to
should / must / can / had better
要加 to
ought to / have to / be able to
Practice F｜必要時填 to，否則填 X
1. You ought ___ know John.
to
2. She had better ___ study hard.
X
3. Kitty had ___ go to Taipei.
to
4. He can ___ play the piano.
X
5. Are you able ___ drive a car?
to
6. You should ___ see a doctor.
X
7. He ought ___ walk around the park.
to
8. You can ___ tell me anything.
X
9. She had better ___ see her teacher.
X

7-7 May I...? / Could I...? / Can I...?

這些句型用來請求允許；please 可放在主要動詞前或句尾。

進階練習
Rule

請求允許

May I + 原形動詞...?Could I + 原形動詞...?Can I + 原形動詞...?
May I borrow your eraser, please?
May I please borrow your eraser?
Note

常見回答

  • Yes, of course / certainly.
  • Sure.
  • That’s okay.
  • I’m sorry.
Practice G｜請重組句子
1. Could / Mandy? / I / to / speak
Could I speak to Mandy?
2. please? / Can / John, / I / to / talk
Can I talk to John, please?
3. computer? / May / use / I / the
May I use the computer?
4. May / please? / a / I / message, / leave
May I leave a message, please?
5. Could / you? / I / help
Could I help you?
6. I / your / please / borrow / book? / Can
Can I please borrow your book?

7-8 Would you...? / Could you...? / Can you...? / Will you...?

這些句型用於禮貌請求對方幫忙。Would / Could 通常比 Can 更正式。

進階練習
Rule

請求幫忙

Would / Could / Can / Will you + 原形動詞...?
Could you open the door?
Would you answer the phone for me?
Note

常見回答

  • Yes, of course / certainly.
  • Sure.
  • I’d like to help.
  • I’d be happy / glad to.
  • I’m sorry.
Practice H｜請依提示回答或改寫
1. Could you give me the number?（please 放句中）
Could you please give me the number?
2. Would you answer the phone for me?（肯定回答）
Yes, of course.
3. Will you turn the CD player down?（please 放句尾）
Will you turn the CD player down, please?
4. Yes, certainly.（請求對方開窗戶）
Could you open the window?
5. Can you pick up your pencil?（please 放句中）
Can you please pick up your pencil?
6. Yes, of course.（請求對方關燈）
Would you turn off the light?
