4-1 所有格代名詞

所有格代名詞用來取代「某人的某物」，避免重複說出名詞。

4-3 不定代名詞 one / ones

one 代替單數可數名詞，ones 代替複數可數名詞，用來避免名詞重複。

基礎練習
Compare

one 與 ones

I don’t like this yellow camera. I like the blue one.
The red cameras are more beautiful than the blue ones.
one
代替單數名詞，例如 the blue one
ones
代替複數名詞，例如 the blue ones
Practice E｜請填入 one 或 ones
1. I need a house. I want to buy ____.
one
2. The big shoes are better than the small ____.
ones
3. I don’t have a ruler. Can I borrow ____ from you?
one
4. This skirt is too fashionable for me. How about that ____?
one
5. They don’t like long novels. How about short ____?
ones
6. These shirts are much nicer than those ____.
ones
7. This lesson is more interesting than that ____.
one

4-4 不定代名詞 one / any

one 與 any 都可以代替單數可數名詞；搭配 of + 限定詞 + 複數名詞時，後面用單數動詞。

基礎練習
Pattern

基本句型

one / any + of + the / these / those / my + 複數名詞one / any + of + them / us / you + 單數動詞
I didn’t eat any of those peaches on the table.
One of you has to mop the floor.
Practice F｜請重組句子
1. excellent. / those teachers / One / is / of
One of those teachers is excellent.
2. my / pencils? / Do / want / you / any / of
Do you want any of my pencils?
3. Kitty / friends. / my / one / best / of / is
Kitty is one of my best friends.
4. my father. / of / One / the doctors / is
One of the doctors is my father.
Practice G｜請用所有格代名詞改寫句子
1. He is one of my best friends.
He is a best friend of mine.
2. She is one of her great teachers.
She is a great teacher of hers.
3. Snoopy is one of his dogs.
Snoopy is a dog of his.
4. Harry Potter is one of their favorite books.
Harry Potter is a favorite book of theirs.

4-5 數量代名詞 both / all

both 用於兩者，all 用於三者以上；兩者都可用在「數量詞 + of + 群體」結構。

基礎練習
Pattern

用法整理

  • Both of my sisters are nurses.
  • All of them are teachers.
both + of + 限定詞 / 受格all + of + 限定詞 / 受格
Practice H｜請依提示造句
1. All / my students / great
All of my students are great.
2. All / the peaches / pink
All of the peaches are pink.
3. Both / her parents / engineers
Both of her parents are engineers.
4. Both / them / my classmates
Both of them are my classmates.
5. Both / my sisters / good actresses
Both of my sisters are good actresses.

4-6 數量代名詞 any / some / many / much / most

搭配可數名詞時通常用複數動詞；搭配不可數名詞時則多用單數動詞。

基礎練習
Rule

可數與不可數的動詞變化

Some of the students wear uniforms.
Much of the money was wasted.
Practice I｜請圈出正確的動詞
1. Both of my brothers (wear / wears) uniforms.
wear
2. Much of the milk (are / is) fresh.
is
3. Most of my students (studies / study) hard.
study
4. Many of the boys (is / are) happy.
are
5. Some of the juice (is / are) very sweet.
is
6. Some of the girls (likes / like) Mr. Wang.
like
7. All of the soccer players (is / are) strong.
are

4-7 不定代名詞 the other / another

another 表不特定的另一個；the other 表兩個中的另一個。

進階練習
Compare

兩個關鍵差異

I don’t like this hat. Show me another.
I don’t like this hat. Show me the other.
another
很多個之中的另一個
the other
兩個之中的另一個
Practice J｜請填入 another 或 the other
1. Raise your right hand, and wave ____ hand.
the other
2. Can you close one eye and open ____ one?
the other
3. I only had one cup of coffee. Let’s have ____.
another
4. Put ____ foot down.
the other
5. To know is one thing; to teach is ____.
another

4-8 不定代名詞 one..., another..., and the other...

描述兩個、三個或很多個物品時，可用 one / another / the other 來逐一說明。

進階練習
Pattern

句型比較

There are three hats. One is orange, another is blue, and the other is white.
很多個
one..., and another...
兩個
one..., and the other...
三個
one..., another..., and the other...
Practice K｜請填入 one、another 或 the other
1. She has two bicycles. ____ is red, and ____ is blue.
One / the other
2. There are three bags in the shop. ____ is large, ____ is medium, and ____ is small.
One / another / the other
3. I have three best friends. ____ is Lily, ____ is Sandy, and ____ is Kitty.
One / another / the other
4. Here are two pencils. ____ is long, and ____ is short.
One / the other

4-9 不定代名詞 others / the others

others 表不特定的「其他一些」；the others 表剩下的「其餘那些」。

進階練習
Compare

others 與 the others

In art class, some draw fruits, while the others draw people.
others
不特定的其他一些
the others
特定、剩下的其他那些
Practice L｜請翻譯下列句子
1. 我不喜歡這些裙子。你還有其中的另一些嗎？
I don’t like these skirts. Do you have other ones?
2. 我不喜歡這些長的襯衫。我想要剩下的另一些短襯衫。
I don’t like these long shirts. I want the other short ones.
3. 我不喜歡這些紅色的夾克。給我看看剩下的另一些夾克。
I don’t like these red jackets. Show me the others.
4. 我不喜歡這些圓桌。給我看看其他的另一些桌子。
I don’t like these round tables. Show me others.

4-10 one..., and the others... / some..., and the others... / one..., and others... / some..., and others...

another / the other 與 others / the others 可以組合成四種常見句型，用來表達「一個／一些」與「其他」的關係。

進階練習
Pattern

四種用法

  • one..., and the others：一個，其餘全部
  • some..., and the others：一些，其餘全部
  • one..., and others：一個，另一些（不是全部）
  • some..., and others：一些，另一些
用法單數複數
不特定anotherothers
特定the otherthe others
Practice M｜請翻譯下列句子
1. 桌上有很多枝鉛筆；一枝是紅色的，其他一部分是綠色的。
There are many pencils. One is red, and others are green.
2. 椅子下有很多個橡皮擦；一些是小的，其他一部分是大的。
There are many erasers under the chair. Some are small, and others are big.
3. 黑板上有很多字；一些是中文，其他全部是英文。
There are many words on the blackboard. Some are Chinese, and the others are English.
4. 商店裡有很多錢包；一個是圓型的，其他全部是方型的。
There are many purses in the store. One is round, and the others are square.
5. 這裡有十台 CD 音響；一台是紫色的，其他全部是藍色的。
There are ten CD players here. One is purple, and the others are blue.
