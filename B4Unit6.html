6-1 授與動詞

授與動詞又稱雙賓動詞，常同時接「人」與「物」兩個受詞。改寫時依動詞搭配 to、for、from 或 of。

基礎練習
Double Objects

雙賓句型

  • 接 to：give、send、show、sell、lend、write、read 等。
  • 接 for：buy、make、cook、find 等。
  • ask 常搭配 of；borrow 常搭配 from。
V + 人 + 物V + 物 + to / for / of / from + 人
Jack gave Jackson two pens.
= Jack gave two pens to Jackson.
Why not cook chicken soup for your brother?
Common Verbs

常見授與動詞

to
give / send / show / sell / lend
for
buy / make / cook / find
other
ask ... of / borrow ... from
Practice A｜請填入適當的介系詞
1. My brother gave a bicycle ___ me.
to
2. She teaches English ___ us.
to
3. Peter bought flowers ___ Mandy.
for
4. I borrowed the book ___ the library.
from
5. Eva asked a favor ___ Miss Wang.
of
Practice B｜請加入介系詞改寫授與動詞的句子
1. The man sold Mr. Wu a car.
The man sold a car to Mr. Wu.
2. She gave me a book.
She gave a book to me.
3. He showed them a map.
He showed a map to them.
4. He asked Mr. Wang a question.
He asked a question of Mr. Wang.
5. She lent Patty a bicycle.
She lent a bicycle to Patty.
6. Kitty sent me a gift.
Kitty sent a gift to me.

6-2 花費 take 的用法

take 表「花費時間」，可使用虛主詞 it、動名詞片語或人作主詞。how long 用來詢問時間長度。

基礎練習
Take Time

三種句型

It takes / took + 人 + 時間 + to VV-ing + takes / took + 人 + 時間人 + take(s) / took + 時間 + to V
It takes me two hours to do my homework every day.
To do my homework takes two hours every day.
I take two hours to do my homework every day.
Question

How long 問句

How long + does / did + it + take + 人 + to V?How long + do / does / did + 人 + take + to V?
How long does it take you to do your homework?
Practice C｜請翻譯下列句子（每題三種寫法）
1. Jack 花了三小時彈鋼琴。
It took Jack three hours to play the piano. / Playing the piano took Jack three hours. / Jack took three hours to play the piano.
2. Mandy 每天花兩小時洗碗盤。
It takes Mandy two hours to do the dishes every day. / Doing the dishes takes Mandy two hours every day. / Mandy takes two hours to do the dishes every day.
3. Kate 和 Kitty 每天花一小時散步。
It takes Kate and Kitty one hour to take a walk every day. / Taking a walk takes Kate and Kitty one hour every day. / Kate and Kitty take one hour to take a walk every day.
4. Jeff 花了四小時打網球。
It took Jeff four hours to play tennis. / Playing tennis took Jeff four hours. / Jeff took four hours to play tennis.
Practice D｜請依提示造原問句
1. It took Jeff four hours to go to Hsinchu.
How long did it take Jeff to go to Hsinchu?
2. Sandy takes two hours to mop the floor every day.
How long does Sandy take to mop the floor every day?
3. Mom takes three hours to shop for the picnic.
How long does Mom take to shop for the picnic?
4. It takes John two hours to study English.
How long does it take John to study English?
5. It took my brother one hour to take a shower.
How long did it take your brother to take a shower?

6-3 花費 cost 的用法

cost 表「花費金錢」，主詞通常是物或虛主詞 it，不使用人作主詞。how much 用來詢問金額。

基礎練習
Cost Money

cost 的句型

It costs / cost + 人 + 錢 + to V物 + costs / cost + 人 + 錢
It cost me two hundred dollars to pay the bill.
To pay the bill cost me two hundred dollars.
Question

How much 問句

How much + does / did + 物 / it + cost (+ 人)?
How much does the chair cost? → It costs three hundred dollars.
Practice E｜請翻譯下列句子
1. Jack 花了三千元買一台相機。
It cost Jack three thousand dollars to buy the camera. / The camera cost Jack three thousand dollars.
2. 母親每天花五百元買蔬菜水果。
It costs Mom five hundred dollars to buy vegetables and fruit every day. / Vegetables and fruit cost Mom five hundred dollars every day.
3. 我每天花八十元搭乘火車。
It costs me eighty dollars to take a train every day. / Taking a train costs me eighty dollars every day.
4. Jeff 花了五千元買這台 CD 音響。
It cost Jeff five thousand dollars to buy the CD player. / The CD player cost Jeff five thousand dollars.

6-4 花費 spend 的用法

spend 後可接時間或金錢，主詞使用人；後面接 V-ing 或 on + 名詞。

基礎練習
Spend

spend 的基本句型

人 + spend(s) / spent + 時間 / 錢 + V-ing人 + spend(s) / spent + 時間 / 錢 + on + 名詞
I spend two hours doing my homework every day.
I spent 500 dollars buying that watch.
Conversion

與 take / cost 互換

時間
spend + 時間 + V-ing ↔ it takes + 人 + 時間 + to V
金錢
spend + 錢 + V-ing ↔ it costs + 人 + 錢 + to V
Practice F｜請依提示改寫句子
1. It cost Jack three thousand dollars to buy the camera. (spend)
Jack spent three thousand dollars buying the camera.
2. It takes Mom two hours to go to the office every day. (spend)
Mom spends two hours going to the office every day.
3. I spend one hour taking the train every day. (take)
It takes me one hour to take the train every day.
4. Sarah spent thirty thousand dollars buying the refrigerator. (cost)
It cost Sarah thirty thousand dollars to buy the refrigerator.
5. Tina spends one hour practicing dancing every day. (take)
It takes Tina one hour to practice dancing every day.
Practice G｜請填入 how long 或 how much
1. ___ does it take to go to Taipei?

How long
5. ___ did it cost Kitty to buy the ticket of the amusement park?
How much
6. ___ did it take Sandy to do the dishes?
How long
Practice H｜請填入 how long 或 how much
1. A: ___ does Mom spend on tickets? B: One thousand dollars.

6-5 forget / stop 的用法

forget 與 stop 後接 V-ing 或 to V 時，語意不同。

基礎練習
Forget

forget + V-ing / to V

I forgot mailing the letter.
I forgot to mail the letter.
Stop

stop + V-ing / to V

The man got lost and stopped to read the map.
Practice I｜請合併下列句子
1. I stopped eating. / I worked.
I stopped to work.
2. She forgot to do it. / She didn’t mail the letter.
She forgot to mail the letter.
3. Kitty was doing her homework. / Kitty stopped it.
Kitty stopped doing her homework.
4. Sandy forgot doing it. / She mopped the floor.
Sandy forgot mopping the floor.
5. Jeffery forgot to do it. / He didn’t brush his teeth.
Jeffery forgot to brush his teeth.
6. Ken stopped doing it. / He quit smoking.
Ken stopped smoking.

6-6 used to / be used to

used to + 原形動詞表過去習慣；be used to + V-ing 表現在習慣做某事。

進階練習
Past Habit

used to + V

主詞 + used to + 原形動詞主詞 + didn’t use to / used not to + 原形動詞
My father used to drive to work.
Current Habit

be used to + V-ing

主詞 + be + used to + V-ing主詞 + be + not + used to + V-ing
My mother is used to drinking water before breakfast.
Practice J｜請依提示造句
1. Tiffany / not / used to / get up early
Tiffany didn’t use to get up early.
2. Tina / be used to / eat sandwiches for lunch
Tina is used to eating sandwiches for lunch.
3. Fiona / used to / do homework / well
Fiona used to do homework well.
4. William / be used to / study English / hard
William is used to studying English hard.
5. Kevin / not / be used to / go to bed / late
Kevin is not used to going to bed late.
6. Karen / not / used to / take the bus / to school
Karen didn’t use to take the bus to school.

6-7 不可分的動詞片語

不可分動詞片語只能把受詞放在整個片語之後，不能插入動詞與介系詞之間。

進階練習
Inseparable

常見不可分片語

片語意思
get on / get off上車 / 下車
talk about / worry about談論 / 擔心
look at / look for / look after看著 / 尋找 / 照顧
laugh at / deal with嘲笑 / 處理
leave for / listen to / wait for前往 / 聆聽 / 等待
Let’s get on the bus. ✓
Let’s get the bus on. ✗
Position

受詞放在哪裡？

不論受詞是名詞或代名詞，都放在完整片語之後。

Practice K｜請重組下列句子
1. is / the rat. / The cat / looking at
The cat is looking at the rat.
2. They / the test. / talking about / are
They are talking about the test.
3. The nurse / the sick people. / looks after
The nurse looks after the sick people.
4. The students / their / are / waiting for / teacher.
The students are waiting for their teacher.

6-8 可分的動詞片語

可分片語的名詞受詞可放在片語後或兩字之間；若受詞是代名詞，必須放在兩字之間。

進階練習
Separable

常見可分片語

片語意思
turn on / turn off打開 / 關掉
put on / take off穿上 / 脫掉
try on / pick up試穿 / 撿起
give up / find out放棄 / 找出
take out / clean up / put down拿出 / 清理 / 放下
Can you turn on the light?
= Can you turn the light on?
Pronoun Rule

代名詞一定放中間

She picked the garbage up. ✓
She picked it up. ✓
She picked up it. ✗
Practice L｜請改寫以下句子
1. I tried on the skirt.
I tried the skirt on.
2. Mom is cleaning up the kitchen.
Mom is cleaning the kitchen up.
3. Would you put down the boxes?
Would you put the boxes down?
4. You need to put on your coat.
You need to put your coat on.
5. Students found out the answers.
Students found the answers out.
6. Don’t give up your life goal.
Don’t give your life goal up.

6-9 動詞片語接名詞或代名詞

可分動詞片語接名詞時有兩種位置；接代名詞時只能放在動詞與副詞之間。

進階練習
Object Placement

名詞與代名詞的位置

She handed in her homework.
= She handed her homework in.
= She handed it in.
Practice M｜請翻譯下列句子
1. 男孩脫掉了他的夾克。
The boy took off his jacket. / The boy took his jacket off. / The boy took it off.
2. 那女孩要我寫下她的名字。
The girl wanted me to write down her name. / The girl wanted me to write her name down. / The girl wanted me to write it down.
3. 請把你的書本收好。
Please put away your books. / Please put your books away. / Please put them away.
